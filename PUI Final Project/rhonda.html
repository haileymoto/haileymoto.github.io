What is Rheumatroid Arthritis?

Rheumatoid arthritis (RA) is an autoimmune disease in which the body’s immune system mistakenly attacks the joints, causing the tissue that lines the joints to thicken.

Common Symptoms

•Tender and swollen joints
•Joint stiffness
•Fatigue and loss of appetite
•Emotional changes and depression
