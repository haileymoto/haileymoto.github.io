Digesting the Problem

Recent research has shown that certain foods can help to alleviate symptoms and, in some cases, reverse progression of various autoimmune and chronic diseases.

As prevalence and cost of autoimmune diseases rise, knowing what foods to eat and what foods to avoid is a cost-effective and delicious way to achieve a better quality of life.
